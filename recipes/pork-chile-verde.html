Pork Chile Verde

Ingredients

  • 4 green bell peppers
  • 1 lb pork butt
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 can chipotles in adobo
  • Fish Sauce (to taste)
  • Cilantro (to taste)

Steps

  1. cut peppers, pork, and onion into 1-inch sized pieces.
  2. place cut ingredients into instant pot, starting with the peppers.
  3. set pressure cooker to "manual" setting for 15 minutes
  4. once timer has finished, depressurize pot and remove pork pieces.
  5. blend remaining vegetables until smooth using an immersion blender
  6. add pork back in, season to taste w/cilantro & fish sauce.
