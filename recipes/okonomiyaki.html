Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 medium egg
  • Taiwanese Sausage (optional)
  • 1/2 knob ginger, grated
  • okonomiyaki sauce
  1. Combine grated cabbage, flour, and egg in mixing bowl
  2. Add water to improve consistency
  3. Oil a pan over medium heat
  4. Add mixture, cooking until set in the middle.
